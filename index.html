About the free milonguero gathering

Donation based. Limited spots.

Abrazo de Arrabal is a free milonguero gathering (donation) for a small group. During two days we will share workshops, guided practices and social moments to rediscover the essentials of tango: connection, communication and the pleasure of dancing together.

The program includes

Saturday: 2hs workshop: walking, connection (abrazo) and impro
Sunday: 2hs workshop: musicality and impro and a guided practice "One more tanda" with the option of feedback.

Level or experience do not matter. Workshops in Spanish, Gemand English and/or French.

Next gathering: September 27 and 28 in Berlin

About us

We are Romina and Ramón

A tango couple united by the embrace and by a shared way of feeling life. For us tango is more than a dance. It is connection, listening and emotion in movement.

Romina

I am a tango dancer born in the western area of Greater Buenos Aires. I started dancing 10 years ago at Club Premier in Caballito. My style is joyful and dynamic, with influences from theatre, pilates and Latin rhythms. Dancing connects me with the present moment and with the partner I share the floor with.

Tango saved my life. Today, together with Ramón, we share a program that unites passion, fun and a deep view on connection.

Ramón

I discovered dance through folklore and Latin rhythms and then fell in love with tango. Since 2000 I have dedicated myself to perfecting it professionally. I have been awarded in competitions in Argentina and Chile. I have been teaching since 2005 and I direct the association mitango et caetera in France.

Tango is my favourite dance. Other dances helped me shape my style and understand the body and connection better.
